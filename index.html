On the day

There won’t be a ceremony on the day, as we’ll be having a small, intimate ceremony in Camden earlier in the month. You can expect canapés, champagne, speeches and plenty of dancing.

We’ll be serving a wedding feast that you’re welcome to enjoy at your leisure between 5 and 7pm, rather than a formal sit-down meal, so you can eat, drink and celebrate in your own time.


Dress Code

Please wear whatever you will feel comfortable in for a late September's day and night. The grounds of Manor Farmhouse are mostly gravel and grass so we advise wearing appropriate shoes.
